1. How to Connect

The BattleBox AI is a private GPT that connects directly to your Kabroda account data. It does not require copying or pasting.

  1. Click "Launch AI Command" from the Suite.
  2. In ChatGPT, click "Start" or type a command like "Run Numbers".
  3. You will see a button: "Sign in with kabroda.com".
  4. Click it to authorize the secure link. This verifies your membership.
