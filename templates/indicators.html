⚡ How to Sync Your Charts

Step 01

Open each script on TradingView and add it to your Favorites (Star icon).

Step 02

Open your chart. Go to Indicators → Favorites and add Kabroda BattleBox Command. Add KTBB Intel if desired.

Step 03

Go to Suite and click "Copy Indicator Data". Open the indicator settings and paste the values into the "Session Data" field.

If levels do not update, confirm the symbol and refresh the chart.
