/// TOPOLOGY MAP
1. THE TRIGGERS (ENTRY)
The "Line in the Sand" where 15m momentum shifts. We never front-run the trigger. We wait for the price to cross.
2. THE DAILY WALL (RESISTANCE/SUPPORT)
Concrete. Price respects this 80% of the time. This is always TARGET 1 for Magnet trades.
3. THE RUNWAY (THE GAP)
The empty space between #1 and #2. If this space is empty (0.5%+), we trade. If it is < 0.5%, we sit.
