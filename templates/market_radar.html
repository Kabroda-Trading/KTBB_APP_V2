INTELLIGENCE ACADEMY

The Radar classifies the gap between the trigger line and the major daily wall into operational tiers.

SNIPER (L) /
SNIPER (S)
Extreme precision execution. Trigger is directly touching the Daily EMA (within 0.5%) AND fully aligned with momentum. Bypasses normal gap rules. High win-rate anomaly.
MAGNET (PRIMAL ZONE)
The optimal mathematical gap. Big enough to be profitable, tight enough to act as a localized magnet pulling price to the wall.
EXTENDED MAGNET
The gap exceeds optimal Primal size, but hasn't reached full Exhaustion yet. Probability of hitting the wall drops. Secure profits early.
JAILBREAK
Triggers are inverted (Breakout is ABOVE resistance / Breakdown is BELOW support). Price is breaking into open air. No walls exist. Trail stops.
DEATH ZONE (CHOP/EXHAUST)
Gap is mathematically unviable. Either too small (creates chop/wicks) or too large (requires more than daily ATR to clear). Do not trade.
